KASARAGOD



Nestled in the northernmost district of Kerala, India, lies the picturesque town of Kasaragod. Blessed with serene beaches, lush greenery, and a rich cultural heritage, Kasaragod is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. Explore the historic Bekal Fort, standing majestically atop a hill overlooking the Arabian Sea, offering panoramic views of the coastline. Indulge in the tranquil beauty of Bekal Beach, where golden sands meet turquoise waters, perfect for a relaxing getaway. Delve into the vibrant traditions of the region at the Theyyam performances, showcasing the colorful folklore and rituals of Kerala. With its captivating natural landscapes and cultural treasures, Kasaragod beckons travelers to immerse themselves in its timeless charm. Experience the allure of Kasaragod and uncover the hidden wonders of this idyllic destination.
