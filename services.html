Services

Our travel website GoKerala offers a comprehensive range of services designed to enhance your travel experience in Kerala. From accommodation booking with options ranging from budget hostels to luxury resorts, to a variety of transportation services including airport transfers and car rentals, we ensure your travel logistics are seamless.

Accommodation Booking

We Offer a range of accommodation options from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious resorts. Provide detailed descriptions, photos, amenities, user reviews, and ratings to help travelers make informed choices. Ensure seamless booking processes with secure payment options and instant confirmation.

Transportation Services

We Provide various transportation options, including airport transfers, car rentals, and local transportation services. Offer detailed information on the types of vehicles available, pricing, and additional services like chauffeurs or guided tours to ensure hassle-free travel within Kerala.

Tour Packages

We Curate a variety of tour packages catering to different interests and budgets. Include cultural tours, adventure trips, wildlife safaris, and heritage site visits. Each package should have detailed itineraries, inclusions, exclusions, and booking options.

Travel Planning

We Assist travelers in planning their trips with personalized itineraries, travel tips, and recommendations for the best times to visit specific attractions. Provide resources like maps, local insights, and packing lists to ensure travelers are well-prepared for their journey.

Customer Support

We Offer 24/7 customer support through multiple channels, including phone, email, and live chat. Provide assistance with bookings, travel queries, emergencies, and other travel-related issues to ensure a smooth travel experience for your customers.

Special Deals and Discounts

We Feature special deals and discounts on accommodations, tours, and transportation services. Highlight seasonal offers, early bird discounts, last-minute deals, and exclusive promotions for members or repeat customers to attract and retain travelers.

Houseboat Cruises

We Offer serene and picturesque houseboat cruises through Kerala's backwaters. Provide options for day cruises, overnight stays, and luxury houseboats with detailed itineraries, onboard amenities, dining options, and booking facilities.

Local Guide Services

We Connect travelers with knowledgeable local guides who can enhance their travel experience with in-depth information about destinations, culture, and history. Offer guides who speak various languages and specialize in different types of tours, such as historical walks, nature treks, or culinary tours.

Photo and Video Services

We Provide professional photography and videography services to capture memorable moments during the trip. Offer packages for individual travelers, couples, families, and groups, with options for photo shoots at scenic locations, event coverage, and personalized travel documentaries.
