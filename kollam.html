KOLLAM



Situated along the tranquil shores of the Arabian Sea, Kollam, also known as Quilon, is a captivating coastal town in Kerala, India. Steeped in history and renowned for its maritime trade, Kollam is a melting pot of cultures and traditions. Explore the historic remnants of Portuguese and Dutch influence at Thangassery, where ancient churches and lighthouses stand as testaments to its colonial past. Embark on a serene journey along the picturesque backwaters of Ashtamudi Lake, dotted with traditional houseboats and lush greenery, offering a glimpse into Kerala's tranquil countryside. Indulge in the vibrant flavors of Kollam's seafood delicacies, tantalizing your taste buds with fresh catches from the Arabian Sea. With its scenic beauty, rich heritage, and warm hospitality, Kollam invites travelers to experience the essence of coastal Kerala. Discover the charm of Kollam and create unforgettable memories amidst its serene surroundings.
